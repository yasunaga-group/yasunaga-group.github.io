Summary

I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Mathematical and Computing Sciences, Tokyo Institute of Technology,
supported by JSPS Global COE program: Computationism as a Foundation for the Sciences (CompView).

My research interests are in coding theory, cryptography, and computational complexity theory.
Especially, pseudorandomness in coding theory and cryptography, rationality in cryptography.
